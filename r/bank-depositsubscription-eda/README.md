## Objective
Explore customer and campaign characteristics to understand which factors
are associated with subscription to a term deposit.

## Dataset
Marketing campaign data containing customer demographics, contact history,
and economic indicators.

## Key Questions
- Which customer characteristics are associated with subscription?
- How do campaign variables influence outcomes?
- Are there clear patterns that can inform targeting?

## Process
- Data cleaning and formatting
- Descriptive statistics
- Exploratory visualisation using ggplot2
- Measures of association
- Regression-based exploration

## Key Insights
- Subscription likelihood varies significantly by contact method and campaign timing
- Certain customer segments show consistently higher response rates
- Exploratory analysis informed variable selection for modeling

## Tools Used
R (tidyverse, ggplot2)
